🛒 Blinkit Executive Dashboard
📌 Project Description
The Blinkit Executive Dashboard is an interactive Tableau dashboard developed to analyze inventory, sales, marketing campaigns, and delivery performance. It provides executives with a comprehensive overview of key business metrics, enabling data-driven decisions related to stock management, product performance, campaign effectiveness, and revenue generation.

🎯 Objectives
Monitor overall business performance through KPIs.
Track monthly stock received and customer orders.
Analyze delivery status distribution.
Identify damaged products and stock availability.
Evaluate marketing campaign performance.
Compare revenue generated across different channels.
Understand category-wise profit margins.

📈 Key Insights
Seasonal variations in stock received and customer orders.
Product categories with higher profit margins.
Distribution of successful and delayed deliveries.
Revenue contribution from different marketing channels.
